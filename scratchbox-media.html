FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

How much does it cost to work with ScratchBox?+

Every project is different, so we don't have fixed pricing. We prefer to have a conversation first to understand your goals, then propose a scope and budget that makes sense for you. Book a free 30-min call to get started with no obligation.

How long does a typical project take?+

Timelines vary by project type. A brand identity takes 3–5 weeks, a website 4–8 weeks, and an ongoing social media retainer is monthly.

Do you work with early-stage startups?+

Absolutely — startups are our sweet spot. We've helped businesses go from zero brand presence to market-ready in weeks.

Can you handle everything from strategy to execution?+

Yes — that's what makes us unique. One partner for strategy, design, content, development, and marketing. No handoffs, no fragmentation.

What does the free consultation include?+

A genuine 30-min strategy session — not a sales call. We'll review your situation, discuss goals, and share ideas. You'll leave with value regardless of whether we work together.

Pricing questions, answered.

Do I have to commit long-term?
We recommend a minimum of 3 months to see real results, but there's no lock-in. We believe in earning your business every month through results, not contracts.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Absolutely. As your business grows, your plan can grow with it. Upgrades take effect the next billing cycle, and downgrades are processed with 30 days notice.
What if I just need one thing?
We also do one-off projects — like a single website, a brand identity, or a video campaign. Book a call and we'll scope it out and give you a fixed-price quote.
Are these prices in INR?
Yes, all prices are in Indian Rupees. We work with clients globally too — for international clients, we price in USD. Book a call and we'll sort out the details.
